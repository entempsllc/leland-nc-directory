Best Beaches Near Leland, NC

One of Leland's best features? Easy access to beautiful North Carolina beaches!

Oak Island - 30 minutes

Beautiful sandy beaches, fishing pier, and historic lighthouse. Great for families.

Wrightsville Beach - 35 minutes

Popular beach with crystal clear water, kayaking, and paddleboarding.

Carolina Beach - 40 minutes

Boardwalk, amusement rides, and great seafood restaurants.

Kure Beach - 45 minutes

North Carolina Aquarium and relaxed beach atmosphere.

Tips for Beach Days

Arrive early on weekends for parking. Bring sunscreen and stay hydrated!
