Complete Guide to Moving to Leland, North Carolina

Leland, North Carolina is a growing community near Wilmington with affordable coastal living. Here's what new residents need to know.

Why Leland?

  • Near Wilmington (easy access to city amenities)
  • Affordable coastal living
  • Growing community with new developments
  • Good Brunswick County schools
  • Easy drive to beautiful NC beaches

Housing

Leland offers more affordable options than nearby Wilmington. New subdivisions are developing, with average home prices around $300K+. Great for first-time buyers and families.

Schools

Leland is served by Brunswick County Schools, including North Brunswick High School, West Brunswick High School, and Leland Middle School.

Beaches Nearby

One of Leland's best features is easy access to beautiful NC beaches:

  • Oak Island - 30 minutes
  • Wrightsville Beach - 35 minutes
  • Carolina Beach - 40 minutes

Community

Leland has a growing community with local shops, restaurants, and community events. The town is perfect for those who want coastal living without Wilmington prices.
